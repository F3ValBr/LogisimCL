Yosys Components Library

The Yosys Components library provides a collection of combinational elements designed to support the structures and routing patterns commonly produced by the Yosys synthesis tool. Although some of these components resemble those found in the standard Gates and Wiring libraries, their behavior and configuration options follow the semantics of the corresponding Yosys internal cells, ensuring faithful reconstruction of synthesized circuits during import.
